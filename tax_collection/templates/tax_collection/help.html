Art Gallery Management System Help Center

Welcome to our Art Gallery Management System Help Center. If you have any questions or issues, please refer to the information below.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Q: How do I create an account?
    A: You can create an account by clicking on the "Create your account" button on the home page.
  • Q: How can I reset my password?
    A: You can reset your password by visiting the "Forgot Password" page and following the instructions.
  • Q: How do I add artworks to my gallery?
    A: To add artworks to your gallery, navigate to the "Add Artwork" section in your account dashboard and follow the instructions.
